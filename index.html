Author Introduction

Hayao Miyazaki was born on January 5, 1941 in Bunkyo ku, Tokyo, Japan. He graduated from theDepartment of Political Economy at the University of Tokyo in 1963. Japanese animator,animation producer, manga artist, animation director, animation screenwriter.

In 1959, Hayao Miyazaki was admitted to Gakushuin University and became a member of theChildren's Literature Club. During his university years, he choreographed and created numerouspuppet shows and manga manuscripts. As graduation approached, Hayao Miyazaki realized thathis manga could not sustain his life, so he abandoned manga and devoted himself to animationproduction.
